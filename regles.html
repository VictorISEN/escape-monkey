Rules

Here's how to play Escape Monkey

  1. Objective : The object of the game is to help the monkey escape from the maze.
  2. Controls : Use the arrow keys to move the monkey through the maze.
  3. Scoring : You earn points by completing the maze quickly. The faster you finish, the more points you score.

Escape Monkey is an exciting maze game that takes you on an adventure through the desert. Join us and help our monkey escape!
