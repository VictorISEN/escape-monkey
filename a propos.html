About Escape Monkey

Escape Monkey is an exciting 2D maze game where you have to help a monkey escape from a complicated maze. Developed by a team of dedicated computer engineering students, our goal is to provide a fun and challenging gaming experience.

The game is constantly evolving, with new features and levels added regularly. Stay tuned for updates and have fun playing Escape Monkey!
